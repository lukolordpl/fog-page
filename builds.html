BUILD I USE (SURVIVOR)


IRON WILL

WINDOWS OF OPPORTUNITY

RESILIENCE

WIRETAP


Since developers decited to nerf Dead Hard perk, and everyone is running Sprint Burst perk, i decited to be not oridinary survivor and have an original build. This is why i play with build that do not include exhaustion perks. This is well balanced build that will make sure you will last till exit gates open.

Because I am not running exhaustion perks, IRON WILL is a perfect perk to choose from, because it can gives you all time benefit while injured. It makes the sounds you make when you are injured quieter, in the same time killer is more likly to do not know, where do you exactly are.

RESILIENCE perk is another kind of perk that gives you all time benefits while injured. Basically you do almost all things 9% faster. It is perfect combination with IRON WILL perk.

Since you do not really have perks to run out of killer quickly, there comes perks like WIRETAP and WINDOWS OF OPPORTUNITY in clutch. Thanks to WIRETAP you can see aura of killer when he comes to your generator, and you can pick safety way out to avoid him. Next, WINDOWS OF OPPORTUNITY will show you auras of all things that can help you manage your escape well.


META BUILDS (SURVIVOR)


SPRINT BURST

FIXATED

VIGIL

INNER HEALING


This is probably the most powerful meta build available for now. It gives so much benefits from exhaustion perk and makes you almost unable to catch up by the killer.

Since Dead Hard is not that good anymore, SPRINT BURST perk shines. This is one of the best perks, because it makes your escape so fast. As soon as you start sprinting, the killers will be left behind.

FIXATED perk and VIGIL perk are here only to make SPRINT BURST perk even more powerful. Thanks to FIXATED perk, you can save your SPRINT BURST for later due to 20% faster walking bonus. On the other hand, VIGIL perk provide you bonus to faster recovery from different status effects - even exhaustion. So even if you use your SPRINT BURST, do not worry, you will get it back soon.

And last perk in this build is INNER HEALING. Because new patch nerfed healing yourself with med-kit and healing time is now 33% longer, this is very nice perk to has. Thanks to this perk, after cleasing totem, you are able to heal yourself in no 24 but in 8 seconds by going into the locker. It is simple as that.



DEAD HARD

BALANCED LANDING

RESILIENCE

ADRENALINE


This is another meta build. We can see more and more people running this build. This is agressive build with more then one exhaustion perk that helps you looping killer and the killer can never be sure what perk are you going to use next.

DEAD HARD and BALANCED LANDING are two exhaustion perks in this build, but because these perks are not that easy to activate, you can basically choose when and which to use.

In order to use DEAD HARD perk you need to be injured, and this is why RESILIENCE perk in this build is very good. It can help you with looping by additional 9% vaulting speed. So somethimes insted of using DEAD HARD you may be able to do one more loop thanks to this perk.

And the last perk in this build is ADRENALINE. Because your role is looping a killer, and most of the time you will be injured, ADRENALINE perk is great, because it can heal you instantly even during chase, and in many cases it can saves your life.
