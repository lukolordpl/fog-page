DEAD BY DAYLIGHT

CONTENT


Dead By Daylight is a survival horror multiplayer game created by Behaviour Interactive video game developer.

In this game you can either play survivor or killer, it is up to you. Keep in mind that maximum players in one game is 5, four survivors and one killer. If you decide to play as survivor, you will be able to play with up to 3 friends of yours, but if you decide to play as killer, you will be alone versus other.

Game goal: If you play as survivor you have to survive of course! In order to escape, you and your teammates have to repair 5 of 7 generators located on map. If you will be lucky enough and complete this goal, next step is to find exit gate, open it and escape! But don't forget to avoid the killer, because he won't show mercy. As a killer, goal is simple... Kill all of them!
