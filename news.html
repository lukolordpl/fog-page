Dead by Daylight steps away from the tea party to reveal The Alice In Wonderland Collection, featuring new Outfits for The Huntress, The Artist, Kate Denson, Nea Karlsson, and Ace Visconti.


Mar 7, 2023

Dead by Daylight’s Tools Of Torment Chapter is here, featuring a new Killer – The Skull Merchant – and two new Survivors, Thalita Lyra and Renato Lyra. The Shelter Woods Map has also been updated to include The Skull Merchant’s Hunting Camp. Purchasing this add-on unlocks 3 exclusive Cosmetics: The Skull Merchant’s Slipshod Prototype, Thalita Lyra’s Golden Ambition, and Renato Lyra’s Golden Touch.


Mar 2, 2023

It’s official. A Dead by Daylight movie is coming. Behaviour Interactive has teamed with Atomic Monster and Blumhouse to develop Dead by Daylight’s feature film adaptation, which will bring the world of The Entity to the big screen for the first time.
